Map:

Patrol spawns are marked with a P. These spawnees will immediately follow a predetermined path.
Dots marked with an i are investigator spawns. These goons will walk towards you if you make any kind of noise.
Entry indicators: A 1 indicates possible spawns for primary entry only, and 2 indicates spawns possible for the secondary entry only.
An X indicates incapacitated spawns.
