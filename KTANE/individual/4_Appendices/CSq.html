Appendix CSq: Identifying brethren of Colored Squares

If one color occurs fewer times than any other (no tie):
(Not) Colored Squares
Upon a successful button press, the pressed button will turn white in Colored Squares and black in Not Colored Squares.
If two colors occur fewer times than the rest (two-way tie):
Uncolored Squares
If three colors occur exactly twice each and two occur exactly 5 times each:
Decolored Squares
If four colors occur exactly once each:
Discolored Squares
If four colors occur exactly three times each:
Varicolored Squares
If there are 16 different colors:
Juxtacolored Squares
